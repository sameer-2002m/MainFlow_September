Are SuperBeat headphones compatible with IOS devices?

Yes, SuperBeat headphones are designed to work seamlessly with a wide range of devices, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and more. They are compatible with both iOS and Android platforms.

Do SuperBeat headphones offer noise-cancellation features?

Absolutely! SuperBeat headphones are equipped with advanced noise-cancellation technology, allowing you to immerse yourself in your music without being disturbed by external sounds.

What is the range of the Bluetooth connectivity on SuperBeat headphones?

SuperBeat headphones typically offer a Bluetooth range of up to 30 feet (approximately 10 meters), ensuring a stable connection even when your device is not right beside you.

Are SuperBeat headphones sweat and water-resistant for workouts?

Some of our models are designed with sweat and water resistance, making them suitable for workouts and outdoor activities. Please check the product specifications for details on specific models.

Are SuperBeat headphones suitable for gaming and watching movies as well as listening to music?

Yes, SuperBeat headphones excel in various entertainment scenarios, offering a wide soundstage and clarity for an immersive gaming and cinematic experience.
